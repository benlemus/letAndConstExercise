1) What is the difference between var and let?
      Var can be redeclared, with let you cannot redeclare.

2) What is the difference between var and const?
      Var can be redelcared and reassign, with const you cannot redeclare or reassign. 

3) What is the difference between let and const?
      Let can be reassigned, with const you cannot reassign.

4) What is hoisting?
      Hoisting is when you use var and can use it before it has been initialized because of how Javascript reads variables first.
